Shadows Die Twice is a 2019 action-adventure game by FromSoftware, the creators of the Dark Souls series. Players take on the role of a disgraced, one-armed warrior known as Wolf, who is tasked with rescuing his lord and exacting revenge on his enemies in a dark, brutal setting inspired by 14th-century Japan.
